
1. Firts, sit down at a lat pulldown station.
2. Grab the bar with an overhand grip that's just beyond shoulder width.
3. Without moving your torso, pull your shoulders back and down.
4. Then bring the bar down to your chest.
5. Pause for 3 sec, then slowly return to the starting position, that's one rep.
6. 8-10 reps per set do 3 sets.
