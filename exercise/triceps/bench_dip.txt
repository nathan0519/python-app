
1. Stand facing away from a bench.
2. Grab it with both hands at shoulder-width. 
3. Extend your legs out in front of you.
4. slowly lower your body by flexing at the elbows until your arm at forearm create a 90-degree angle.
5. Using your triceps lift yourself back to the starting position , that's one rep.
6. 10-15 reps per set do 3 sets.
