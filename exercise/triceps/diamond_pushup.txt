
1. Lower yourself down into a standard plank or press-up position.
2. Bring your hands close to each other at chest level, with your thumbs touching one another.
3. Make sure your forefingers touching. Your spine should be straight.
4. Your core and glutes should be squeezed tight, lower yourself down to the floor.
5. Pause, maintaining the squeeze in your core and glutes, 
6. then push back up to the original position by straightening your arms, that's one rep.
7. 10-15 reps per set do 3 sets.
