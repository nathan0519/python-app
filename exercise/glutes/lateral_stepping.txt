
1. Secure a flat resistance band just above your ankles.
2. Stand with your feet at about hip width, keeping feet forward.
3. Keeping your weight in your heels, step your right foot laterally.
4. Maintaining the tension in the band and keep the band taut as you step your left foot slightly to the right.
5. Continue stepping sideways to your right for about 5 steps.
6. Then step to your left to return to the starting position. Repeat three times.
