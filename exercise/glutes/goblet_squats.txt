
1. Stand with feet at shoulder width, holding a kettlebell at your chest.
2. Then, inhale as you bend your knees and push your hips back to lower into a squat.
3. Aim for your hips to come as low as your knees and avoid tucking your tailbone.
4. Exhale and drive through your heels to rise to standing.
5. 10-12 reps per set, do 3 set.
