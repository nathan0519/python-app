
1. Stand holding dumbbells at your hips, core tight and glutes squeezed.
2. Make sure your shoulder blades tight as well.
3. Your elbows should have a slight bend in them.
4. Without rocking, raise the dumbbells until your wrists are just below your shoulders.
5. Keep the fronts of the dumbbells pointing upwards just slightly.Lower with control, that's one rep.
6. 12-15 reps per set do 3 sets.
