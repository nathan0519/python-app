
1. Stand holding two dumbbells at your shoulders.
2. Tighten your core and glutes.
3. Your elbows should be slightly in front of you. This is the start.
4. Now press the dumbbells upward, straightening your elbows and shoulders.
5. Lower the dumbbells back to your shoulders with control, that's one rep.
6. 8-10 reps per set do 3 sets.
