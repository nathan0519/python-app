
1. Prop your upper back and shoulders on a bench, and hug your arms across your chest.
2. Place your feet flat on the floor, sitting your hips low.
3. Drive both feet into the ground as you press both hips up toward the ceiling.
4. At the top of the movement, squeeze your glutes and hold for two seconds.
5. Then, lower that one rep. Use just your bodyweight, or add weight for a greater challenge.
6. 12-15 reps per set, do 3 sets.
