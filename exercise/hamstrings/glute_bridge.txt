
1. Lie down with your back, palms, and feet flat on the floor, knees bent.
2. Press both feet into the ground as you raise both hips up toward the ceiling.
3. At the top of the movement, squeeze your glutes together to ensure you don’t arch or drop your lower back.
4. Hold at the top for a 2-second count, then lower that one rep.
5. 12-15 reps per set, do 3 sets.
