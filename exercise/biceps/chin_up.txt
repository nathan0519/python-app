
1. Grab a chinup bar using a shoulder-width underhand grip and hang at arm’s length.
2. Then, squeeze your shoulder blades down and back.
3. Bend your elbows, and pull the top of your chest to the bar.
4. Hold for 3 seconds, and slowly lower your body back to the starting position, that's one rep.
5. 10 reps per set do 3 sets.
