
1. Lie your face down on the ground and stretch both arms out in front of the body.
2. Keeping the legs stretched out and flat on the ground. 
3. Then, raise both the hands and feet.
4. Aiming to create a gap of about 6 inches between them and the floor. 
5. Try to pull in the bellybutton, lifting it off the floor to engage the core muscles. 
6. Keep the head straight and look at the floor to avoid neck injury. 
7. Stretch the hands and feet outward as far as possible.
8. Hold the position for 2 sec return to the starting position, that's one rep.
9. 10 reps per set do 3 sets.
