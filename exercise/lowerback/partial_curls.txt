
1. Lie your back on the floor and bend the knees.
2. Keeping the feet flat and hip-width apart.
3. Cross the hands over the chest and breathe in deeply.
4. On the breath out, engage the abdominal muscles by pulling in the stomach. 
5. Gently raise the head and shoulders 2 inches off the ground while keeping the neck in line with the spine.
6. Hold for 5 sec then return to the starting position, that's one reps.
7. 10 reps per set do 3 sets.
