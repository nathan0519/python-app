
1. Sit up straight on the leg extension machine.
2. Imagine you have a seat belt around your waist.
3. Pulling your hips down into the seat.
4. Then, use your quads to initiate the movement and avoid “kicking” the weight up.
5. Extend your legs fully and pause for one second at the top, then lower under control that's one rep.
6. 12-15 per reps, do 2 sets.
