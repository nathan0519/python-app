
1. Put your hands flat on the floor or the wall.
2. Then, lower your body toward your hands while keeping your back straight and your stomach tight. 
3. Do not let your head drop, keep your neck in line with the rest of your spine.
4. Lower your body until you’re close to the floor or the wall.
5. Then push back into an upright position, that's one rep.
6. Inhale as you go down and exhale as you push up.
7. 10-15 reps per set do 3 sets.
