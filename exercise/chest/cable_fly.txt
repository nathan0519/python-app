
1. Attach two stirrup handles to the high-pulley cables of a cable-crossover station.
2. Then, grab a handle with each hand and stand in a staggered stance in the middle of the station. 
3. Your arms should be outstretched but slightly bent.
4. Lean forward slightly at your hips; don’t round your back. 
5. Without changing the bend in your arms, bring your hands together. 
6. Slowly reverse the movement, that one rep.
7. 10-15 reps per set do 3 sets.
